How to Make an Easy Origami Ice cream

These instructions will show you how to make an easy origami Ice cream.

  1. Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

  2. Fold both sides in the the centre along the dotted lines.

  3. Fold the top layers of paper out along the dotted lines.

  4. Fold the top layers of paper behind the model along the dotted lines.

  5. Turn the paper over.

  6. Fold the paper in along the dotted lines.

  7. Make 2 Pleat Folds along the dotted lines.

  8. Turn the paper over.

  9. The complete ice cream cone.
