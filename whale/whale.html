How to Make an Easy Origami Whale

These instructions will show you how to make an easy origami Whale.

  1. Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

  2. Fold the bottom of the paper up along the dotted line.

  3. Turn the paper over.

  4. Fold the top and bottom in along the dotted lines.

  5. Fold the top and side in along the dotted lines then turn the paper over.

  6. Fold the tail up a little bit.

  7. The complete whale.
